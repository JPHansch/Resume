For more information on my insurance and actuarial background, visit my Professional Experience page or LinkedIn page using the link below.

I would describe myself as process-oriented and goal-focused, with experience spanning several business units within insurance companies and spanning several countries, from North America to EMEA to Asia Pacific. My main interest lies at the intersection of insurance solutions, streamline processing, and product development. That's why I've decided to enhance my skillset and learn web development. My goal is quite simple: Make insurance solutions and insights simple and readily available for those who want to protect what they truly love.

My hobbies outside of my profession include personal real estate investment, personal finance organization and planning, home-cooking and baking, Seattle Wiffleball, tennis, and resource replacement boardgames.
